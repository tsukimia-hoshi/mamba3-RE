From the search, we identify erf(x) as the most performant point-wise function. The error function erf(·) is
closely related to the cumulative distribution function (CDF) of a standard Gaussian distribution. Specifically,

where both the shift s and the scale α are learnable scalars. γ and β are learnable per-channel vectors. To
integrate Derf into a transformer-based architecture, we replace each normalization layer with a corresponding
